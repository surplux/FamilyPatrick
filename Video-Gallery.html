Faq

Here are the most commonly asked questions by our customers.

Can I join the Team?

Of course, if you have an eye for design, photo and graphics then you can be part of our small team. We essentially work with Photoshop, Lightroom, Premier pro, Davinci and other secondary softwares. and what more...you can work from anywhere. Use our contact form.

How do i make a booking?

To make a booking , go to our contact page and use our contact form and we will respond to you as within 24hours

How do I know your price?

We only reveal our prices on request. Probably when we have a general overview of the event we can then work the price with the event requiremtn.

What if  I do not like my final photos and videos?

Well, We are dedicated to giving you the best, so we would ask  before the event your preferences so we all be in sync. If however we did not deliver our part, then we can negotiate a certain level of compensation or retouch.
